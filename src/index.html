🚫 Never share your OTP, TAC, PIN, or password with anyone over the phone
🏦 Banks will never ask you to transfer money to a "safe account"
📱 Never install TeamViewer, AnyDesk, or any APK a caller asks you to
👮 Police and LHDN will never threaten arrest over the phone or demand immediate payment
🤫 If a caller says "don't tell anyone about this call" — it's a scam
📞 When in doubt, hang up and call the bank's official hotline yourself
💰 No legitimate investment guarantees 30% monthly returns — that's always a scam
❤️ If an online partner you've never met asks for money — stop and verify
